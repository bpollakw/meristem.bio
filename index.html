Vision

We aim for tomorrow. Today, more than ever, we need better and quicker ways to enhance our fruit cultivars for a more sustainable fruit production paradigm. We need to find ways to improve our plants to increase yield with more efficient water and nutrient use, to be more nutricious and healthy, and to provide longer-lasting shelflife.

In Meristem, we are developing the techniques and tools we need for making that future happen. State of the art molecular and sequencing techniques, robotics and automation platforms, artificial intelligence and data integration will pave the way to develop next-generation fruits cultivars.
